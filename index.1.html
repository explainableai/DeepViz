Visualizing What Deep Neural Network Learn

Demonstrates the internal workngs of a Convolutional Neural Network (Convnet).

  1. Intermediate Activations
    Visualizing intermediate convnet outputs. Understand how successive convnet layers transform their input, and see what each filters are sensitive to.
  2. Filters
    Visualizing convnets filters is useful to understand precisely what visual pattern or concept each filter in a convnet is receptive to.
  3. Class Activation Heatmaps
    Visualizing heatmaps of class activation in an image and understand which part of an image attributed most to the classification decision.
